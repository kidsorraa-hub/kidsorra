🌈 The Kidsorra Method

A New Way To Help Children Grow

Kidsorra is not just an English program. It is a complete child development system designed to build confidence, communication, creativity and emotional intelligence through joyful learning.

After four years of research by a multidisciplinary team including a psychologist, an NLP instructor, and a computer engineer, Kidsorra was developed as a practical growth system for children.

Before becoming a complete educational program, Kidsorra was tested in real classrooms for more than two years. The program was continuously refined through observation, feedback and professional evaluation.

🧠

  • ✔ SEL
